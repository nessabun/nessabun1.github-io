Book Cover

Tool(s): Adobe Firefly, Photoshop, ChatGPT

Project Description: I used ChatGPT to generate a title for my book cover. I used Adobe Firefly to generate an image for my book cover using a prompt I made up. The image is based off of your average romance book cover. I used Photoshop to add the title, subtitle and author to the image.

Fantasy romance involving a heroine with a crystal heart
